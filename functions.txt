
== Functions ==

1) Functions should one and only one thing.

2) Follow the code-name structure.

3) Simpler names are more readable and more understandable.

4) Choose a name who exactly defines what the function is doing.

5) The function should be small, usually smaller than you thing.

6) Functions shouldn't have side effects.

7) Functions should have a small number of parameters, no more than four and even four should be avoided.

8) Don't use flag arguments.

9) Declare them close to their usage.
